    • Ticker: URBC
    • Symbol: URBC – URBC
    • Proof-of-Work algorithm: CryptoNight
    • Block time: 60 seconds
    • Difficulty: retarget each block
    • Block reward: slight decrease every block R = (S - A)/218

    • Divisible: up to 608 atomic units (up to 8 decimal places)
    • Emission: ∞, initial supply of 60000000,00000000 atomic units, or 60 million URBC.
    • Tail emission: ~2% inflation per year.
